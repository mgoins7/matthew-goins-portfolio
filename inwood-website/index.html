Our story

Same table. Same recipes. Since 1972.

"It's clean, it's simple, and it's just about the same as it was fifty years ago — that's the point."

The Inwood opened its doors in 1972, and for more than fifty years it's been the place Gordonsville comes for breakfast before work, lunch on a long day, and a full plate on the way home. Travelers find it off Route 15; regulars never left.

We hand-cut our steaks, bake our own biscuits and pies, and keep the soup & salad bar stocked all day. The portions are big, the prices are honest, and the coffee's always on. Pull up a chair — you're family here.
